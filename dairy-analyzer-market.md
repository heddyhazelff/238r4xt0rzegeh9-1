<p><p>The dairy analyzer market primarily consists of two key types: ultrasonic and infrared. Ultrasonic analyzers employ sound waves to measure the composition of dairy products, providing insights into parameters like fat, protein, and moisture content. In contrast, infrared analyzers utilize light absorption principles to analyze the chemical makeup of dairy, enabling rapid and accurate assessments. Both technologies enhance the quality control process in dairy production, ensuring compliance with industry standards and improving product consistency.</p></p>
