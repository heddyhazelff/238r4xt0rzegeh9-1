<p><strong>The Parts Washer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water/Aqueous-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parts washer market is primarily categorized into solvent-based and water/aqueous-based systems. Solvent-based washers use organic solvents to dissolve and remove oils, greases, and contaminants, offering effective cleaning for heavy-duty applications. However, they may pose environmental and health risks. In contrast, water/aqueous-based washers utilize water combined with detergents or surfactants, providing a safer and more environmentally friendly option. These systems are suitable for lighter cleaning tasks and are increasingly favored due to stricter regulations on solvent use.</p></p>
